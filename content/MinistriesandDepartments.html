Ministries & Departments

At our church, every ministry and department works together to create an environment where people can grow spiritually, build meaningful relationships, and encounter God in a life-changing way. From worship and discipleship to hospitality, outreach, media, and operations, each team plays a vital role in serving our church family and community with excellence, love, and purpose.

Church Operations & Administration

This department oversees the day-to-day coordination and organization of church activities, ensuring that all ministries and operations run smoothly, efficiently, and in alignment with the church’s vision.

Service Coordination

The Service Coordination team helps organize and manage the flow of church services, supporting a seamless worship experience through scheduling, communication, and service execution.

Church Project & Building

This team oversees church facilities, building projects, renovations, and maintenance efforts to create safe, functional, and welcoming spaces for worship and ministry.

Finance Department

The Finance Department manages offerings, budgeting, financial records, and stewardship processes with integrity, accountability, and transparency.

Digital Operations

This department supports the church’s technology systems, including Church Center, Planning Center, internet connectivity, devices, software systems, and digital infrastructure.

Welfare

The Welfare team provides care and support to members and families during times of need through compassion, encouragement, and practical assistance.

Hospitality

The Hospitality ministry creates a warm and welcoming environment where everyone feels valued, loved, and at home from the moment they arrive.

Guest Experience

This team includes ushers, greeters, first-time guest care, and the information desk, helping guests and members feel welcomed, informed, and connected.

Security & Safety

The Security & Safety team helps maintain a safe and secure environment through emergency response preparedness, parking coordination, and child safety procedures.

Transportation & Logistics

This department coordinates guest transportation, event logistics, and equipment movement to support church services and special events efficiently.

Sanctuary Keepers

The Sanctuary Keepers team cares for the church environment through sanctuary setup, cleaning coordination, facility upkeep, decorations, and creating an atmosphere of excellence.

Events & Outreaches

This ministry coordinates church-wide events and outreach initiatives that strengthen fellowship, impact the community, and advance the church’s mission.

Special Events Planning

This team organizes conferences, anniversaries, picnics, retreats, and special programs that foster community, celebration, and spiritual growth.

Community Outreach & Evangelism

The Outreach ministry shares God’s love beyond the church walls through evangelism, community service, missions, and compassionate outreach initiatives.

Music & Worship Department

The Worship Department leads the congregation in heartfelt praise and worship, creating an atmosphere where people can encounter God through music and worship arts.

Choir

The Choir ministers through vocal worship, helping lead the congregation into meaningful praise and spiritual reflection during services and special events.

Instrumentalists

The Instrumentalists support worship services through musical excellence and creativity using various instruments to enhance the worship experience.

Community Life & Connections

This ministry helps people build meaningful relationships, grow in community, and stay connected through fellowship, care, and discipleship opportunities.

Men’s Ministry

The Men’s Ministry empowers men to grow spiritually, build strong relationships, and become godly leaders in their homes, church, and community.

Women’s Ministry

The Women’s Ministry encourages women through fellowship, discipleship, mentorship, prayer, and programs that nurture spiritual and personal growth.

Youth & Young Adult Ministry

This ministry helps youth and young adults grow in faith, leadership, purpose, and community through fellowship, mentorship, discipleship, and relevant life discussions.

Teen Ministry

The Teen Ministry serves middle and high school students by creating a safe, engaging, and faith-filled environment where teens can grow spiritually and relationally.

Children Ministry

The Children Ministry provides age-appropriate biblical teaching, worship, and care for nursery and elementary-aged children in a safe and nurturing environment.

Membership Classes & Assimilation

This ministry helps new members and guests connect to the church family through orientation, membership classes, follow-up, and pathways for spiritual growth and involvement.

Ministry & Spiritual Growth

This ministry focuses on spiritual development, leadership growth, discipleship, and equipping believers to mature in their walk with Christ.

Ministers Welfare

This team supports ministers and church workers through encouragement, care, and practical support to help them serve effectively and faithfully.

Ministers Scheduling

The Ministers Scheduling team coordinates ministry assignments and service schedules to ensure organized and effective participation during church services and events.

Workers Training

This ministry equips volunteers and workers through leadership development, training sessions, and skill-building opportunities for effective ministry service.

Discipleship & Bible Study

This ministry helps believers grow deeper in God’s Word through Bible studies, discipleship classes, teaching, and spiritual development programs.

Baptism Coordination

The Baptism Coordination team assists individuals preparing for water baptism and helps facilitate baptism services and spiritual preparation.

Prayer Ministry

The Prayer Ministry intercedes for the church, members, community, and nation while fostering a culture of prayer, spiritual support, and dependence on God.

Media & Communications

The Media & Communications ministry uses technology and creative communication tools to support worship services, outreach efforts, and digital engagement.

Media Technical

This team manages livestreaming, sound, lighting, projection, and visual systems to enhance the in-person and online worship experience.

Photography & Videography

The Photography & Videography team captures church moments, events, and testimonies through creative visual storytelling.

Media Outreach

This ministry manages social media, website content, digital evangelism, announcements, graphic design, and online communication to help share the church’s message locally and globally.
