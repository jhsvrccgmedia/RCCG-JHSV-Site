Giving FAQ

Most asked questions about giving.

Is my gift tax-deductible?

Yes. RCCG Jesus House Silicon Valley is a 501(c)(3) non-profit. We provide annual giving statements for your records.

How do I get a giving statement?

Statements are automatically issued and available through our partner app — Planning Center in January. Email finance@rccgjhsv.org for other giving methods statements if not received by the end of January.

Can I change or pause a recurring gift?

Yes. Sign in at rccgjhsv.churchcenter.com and open Giving from your profile. From there you can edit the amount, change the fund, switch the payment method, or pause and cancel any recurring gift. If you’d rather we handle it, email finance@rccgjhsv.org and we’ll take care of it.

Who can I contact about my giving?

Reach the office at (925) 759-8930 or finance@rccgjhsv.org.
