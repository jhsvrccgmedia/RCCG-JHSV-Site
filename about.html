Our story

A multicultural family of faith, in Silicon Valley.

We are a parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, set in San Jose, gathered around the same simple conviction that brought us all here: that knowing God changes everything.

Our vision

To know God and to make Him known.

Through His Word and His power, in our homes, our workplaces, and across the Bay Area.

Our mission

Making heaven rejoice and transforming the earth.

We are committed to reaching the lost, strengthening believers, raising disciples, and advancing the Kingdom of God with faith, love, and urgency.

What we believe

Rooted in scripture, centered on Christ.

We believe the Bible is God’s Word and the foundation for our faith and life. We believe in one God, eternally revealed as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, our Savior and Lord, who came to redeem humanity, rose from the dead, and will return in glory. We believe in the transforming work of the Holy Spirit, salvation through faith in Christ, and the call to live holy, faithful, and fruitful lives as we prepare for eternity with God.

For the full statement of faith, visit the RCCG statement of faith.
