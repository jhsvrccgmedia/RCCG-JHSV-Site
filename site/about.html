Our story

A multicultural family of faith, in Silicon Valley.

We are a parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, set in San Jose, gathered around the same simple conviction that brought us all here: that knowing God changes everything.

Our vision

To know God and to make Him known.

Through His Word and His power, in our homes, our workplaces, and across the Bay Area.

Our mission

Making heaven rejoice and transforming the earth.

One Sunday, one neighbor, one quiet act of obedience at a time.

What we believe

Rooted in Scripture.

We hold to the historic Christian faith. Scripture as God's revealed Word, the Trinity, salvation by grace through faith in Jesus, the indwelling work of the Holy Spirit, and the Church as a living family of believers.

For the full statement of faith, visit the RCCG global statement. Our preaching, teaching, and ministry flow from this foundation.
