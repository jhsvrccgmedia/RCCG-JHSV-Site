Service length

Sunday School starts at 10:00 AM. The Hours of Transformation service runs from 10:30 AM to about 12:30 PM. We are excited to worship with all, so join us!

Come as you are

Whether your week has been full or quiet, whether you are familiar with church or visiting for the first time, you are welcome here. There is no dress code at Jesus House Silicon Valley. We invite you to arrive comfortably, worship freely, and feel at home among family.

Multicultural, Spirit-filled worship

Our worship is rooted in a rich blend of gospel and contemporary Christian traditions. Whether you lift your voice, sit quietly in reflection, or simply take in the moment, you are welcome to worship in a way that feels sincere and reverent.

Bible teaching that lands

Pastor Asogba's teaching is rooted in Scripture and aimed at how to live it out Monday through Saturday. You'll leave with something to do, not just something to believe.

For the whole family

Learning About God Even in Their Early Years

We want every child to feel welcomed, cared for, and comfortable from the moment they arrive. We help the whole family encounter God.

A Sunday built for them.

Easy drop-off, big worship, stories that stick.

While adults are in the main service, children are cared for in a safe, welcoming space designed just for them. Through age-appropriate Bible teaching, worship, activities, and attentive leaders, they can learn about God in a way that feels joyful and easy to understand. Drop-off and pickup are simple, and every child is checked in with care so families can worship with peace of mind.

A space of their own.

Built for the in-between years.

Our teens have a place where they can ask honest questions, build real friendships, and grow in their faith with leaders who care about them. Through thoughtful conversations, worship, service, and opportunities to participate in church life, middle and high school students are encouraged to discover who they are in Christ and how they can make a difference.

Getting here

Meet with us.

Parking

Free on-site parking is available. Overflow parking is welcome on the surrounding streets. If you need accessibility support, our ushers can meet you at the front entrance.

Where's the entrance?

The main doors face Piercy Road. ADA access is available throughout the building. If you need a hand getting in, message us ahead and an usher will meet you at the curb.

Common questions

Things we get asked.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself. A Bible if you'd like to follow along. Scripture verses are projected during the service.

Will you ask me to do anything?

Please feel free — we invite you to introduce yourself during service and would love to meet you.

Can I watch online instead?

Absolutely. We livestream every Sunday on YouTube and Facebook. The full archive is on our YouTube channel.

What does RCCG stand for?

Redeemed Christian Church of God, a global Christian fellowship. Jesus House Silicon Valley is one of its parishes in the Bay Area.

How do I get connected beyond Sunday?

Fill in the connection card above and we'll reach out. From there, ministries, small groups, and serving teams open up depending on what fits your season.
