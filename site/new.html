For your first Sunday

We saved a seat for you.

Here's everything you need to know before you walk in — service times, what to wear, where to park, what your kids will love.

What to expect

Sunday morning, without surprises.

A simple, welcoming service. About two hours from start to finish. Worship, teaching from God's Word, prayer, and people genuinely glad you came.

01

About 90 minutes

Sunday School begins at 10:00 AM and the Hours of Transformation service runs from 10:30 AM to about 12:30 PM. Come for one or both — there's no wrong way to start.

02

Come as you are

You'll see jeans, traditional dress, and everything in between. There is no dress code at Jesus House — just bring yourself.

03

Multicultural, Spirit-filled worship

A worship band that draws from gospel, contemporary, and African worship traditions. Stand, sit, sing, or just take it in — every response is welcome.

04

Bible teaching that lands

Pastor Asogba's teaching is rooted in Scripture and aimed squarely at how to live it out Monday through Saturday. You'll leave with something to do, not just something to believe.

For your kids

A Sunday built for them.

Children's ministry runs alongside the main service with age-appropriate teaching, songs, and safety protocols. Drop-off and pickup are quick — your kids are in good hands the whole time.

Common questions

Things we get asked.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself. A Bible if you'd like to follow along — but Scripture verses are projected during the service.

What if I'm late?

Walk right in. Sundays at Jesus House are about presence, not punctuality. Ushers will quietly help you find a seat.

Will you ask me to do anything?

No. You won't be asked to stand, give, or introduce yourself. You're welcome to just observe.

Can I watch online instead?

Absolutely. We livestream every Sunday on YouTube and Facebook. The full archive is on our YouTube channel.

What does RCCG stand for?

Redeemed Christian Church of God — a global Christian fellowship. Jesus House Silicon Valley is one of its parishes in the Bay Area.

How do I get connected beyond Sunday?

Fill in the connection card above and we'll reach out. From there, ministries, small groups, and serving teams open up depending on what fits your season.

Still have questions?

Just ask.

There's no question too small. We'd rather hear from you than have you wonder.
