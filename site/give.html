Giving FAQ

A few common questions.

Is my gift tax-deductible?

Yes. RCCG Jesus House Silicon Valley is a 501(c)(3) non-profit. We provide annual giving statements for your records.

How do I get a giving statement?

Statements are issued in January for the prior year. Email rccgjhsv2013@gmail.com if you need yours sooner.

Is online giving secure?

Yes. Online gifts are processed through an industry-standard secure payment platform. Your card details never touch our servers.

Can I change or pause a recurring gift?

Anytime. You can manage your recurring gift directly from the giving portal, or email us and we'll help.

Who can I contact about my giving?

Reach the office at (925) 759-8930 or rccgjhsv2013@gmail.com.
